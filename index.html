About Justin Bieber

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor. Discovered by American talent manager Scooter Braun and signed with RBMG Records in 2008, Bieber gained a massive following with his debut EP, "My World," in 2009. He quickly became a global pop sensation, winning numerous awards and breaking chart records. His music, which spans from pop and R&B to hip hop, has evolved significantly throughout his career, reflecting his personal growth.

Beyond his musical career, Bieber is known for his philanthropy and influence on pop culture. He has used his platform to raise awareness for various causes and has become an influential figure for a generation of fans worldwide.
